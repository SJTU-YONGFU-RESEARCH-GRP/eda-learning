## Fair compare rules

- Report the same metrics on both sides
- Missing reference tools?

## What good looks like
- Educational engines may be slower, that is fine
- Look for legal outputs, stable displacement totals, and plausible HPWL
- Unexplained huge wins usually mean unequal metrics or skipped legality checks
