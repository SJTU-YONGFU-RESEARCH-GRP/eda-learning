Clustering & refinement

  • Affinity metrics
    Rank node pairs by edge-weight and shared-neighbor affinity.
  • Greedy pair merge
    Step heaviest-edge merges down to K clusters and watch cutsize.
  • Size-constrained agglomerative
    Greedy merge with capacity — capacity 2 blocks ABC and raises cut to 8.
  • Label propagation
    Async label updates until communities stabilize.
  • Spectral bisection
    Fiedler vector split of the Laplacian on the five-node graph.
  • Kernighan–Lin
    Pair-swap refinement with rollback from a bad seed (12→3).
  • Fiduccia–Mattheyses
    Single-vertex FM moves with rollback from a bad seed.
  • Multilevel clustering
    Greedy coarsen, project bipartition, FM-refine to P0/P1.
  • Hypergraph clustering
    Pin co-occurrence greedy merge scored by hyperedge cut.
  • Congestion-aware clustering
    Boost edges by λ·congestion, then FM (λ=0 vs λ=5 tradeoff).
  • Timing-aware clustering
    Criticality-weighted FM — plain 3, weighted cut 7.

Partitioning

  • Cutsize & balance
    Report cutsize and balance ratio for bad vs golden bipartitions.
  • Initial bipartition
    Random, greedy, and grow seeds before refinement.
  • KL partition
    Kernighan–Lin from a bad seed (12→3 via A↔D).
  • FM partition
    Fiduccia–Mattheyses single-vertex moves from a bad seed.
  • Spectral partition
    Fiedler-vector spectral bipartition → ABC|DE cut 3.
  • Recursive bisection
    Bisect the largest part until k regions (k=3 → AB|C|DE).
  • Multiway partition
    Recursive k-way vs naive round-robin (cut 8 vs 18).
  • Terminal propagation
    Fixed terminals constrain free nodes via affinity.
  • Hypergraph partition
    Hyperedge-cut FM on a tiny netlist (seed 6 → cut 1).
  • Multilevel partition
    V-cycle stages: coarsen → project → refine → P0/P1.

Placement

  • HPWL metrics
    Half-perimeter wirelength on a tiny 6-cell netlist (starter 52 vs golden 14).
  • Net models
    Clique vs star HPWL on the 4-pin net (16 vs 8 on golden).
  • Force-directed place
    Spring-style force iterations that cut starter HPWL 52→≈18.7.
  • Quadratic place
    Pad-constrained quadratic lite (A,D fixed) → HPWL 48.
  • Analytical place
    Wirelength stage plus density spreading (analytical lite ≈48.1).
  • SA placement
    Few simulated-annealing moves; accept/reject on HPWL (seed 42 → ≈49.6).
  • Density bins
    2×2 bin counts and overflow vs capacity.
  • Spread / legalize lite
    Push overlapping cells apart to a minimum distance of 0.5.
  • Timing-driven place
    Critical-net weighted HPWL (starter 116 vs golden 30).
