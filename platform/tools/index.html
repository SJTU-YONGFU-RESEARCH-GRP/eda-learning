Clustering & refinement

  • Affinity metrics
    Rank node pairs by edge-weight and shared-neighbor affinity.
  • Greedy pair merge
    Step heaviest-edge merges down to K clusters and watch cutsize.
  • Size-constrained agglomerative
    Greedy merge with capacity — capacity 2 blocks ABC and raises cut to 8.
  • Label propagation
    Async label updates until communities stabilize.
  • Spectral bisection
    Fiedler vector split of the Laplacian on the five-node graph.
  • Kernighan–Lin
    Pair-swap refinement with rollback from a bad seed (12→3).
  • Fiduccia–Mattheyses
    Single-vertex FM moves with rollback from a bad seed.
  • Multilevel clustering
    Greedy coarsen, project bipartition, FM-refine to P0/P1.
  • Hypergraph clustering
    Pin co-occurrence greedy merge scored by hyperedge cut.
  • Congestion-aware clustering
    Boost edges by λ·congestion, then FM (λ=0 vs λ=5 tradeoff).
  • Timing-aware clustering
    Criticality-weighted FM — plain 3, weighted cut 7.
